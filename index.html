From Garden To Globe

Our Production Process

A seamless 6-stage journey ensuring uncompromised purity, strict quality control, and timely global dispatch.

Step 01

Farmers

Ethical harvesting of mature cinnamon shoots in southern Sri Lanka.

Step 02

Processing

Artisanal hand-peeling & sun-drying in hygienic facilities.

Step 03

Packaging

Moisture barrier food-grade sealing and grading verification.

Step 04

Warehouse

Climate-controlled storage ensuring essential oil preservation.

Step 05

Export

Phytosanitary inspection, customs clearance & ocean/air freight.

Step 06

Customers

Delivered fresh to spice importers, food brands & global buyers.
