Our Story & Values

About Zelan Cinnamon

Dedicated Sri Lankan exporter of 100% authentic pure Ceylon Cinnamon, setting global benchmarks in agricultural purity.

Sri Lankan Heritage

Pioneering Pure Ceylon Cinnamon Exports

Zelan Cinnamon was founded with a singular objective: to connect international buyers directly with Sri Lanka’s authentic Cinnamomum Verum, known worldwide as "True Cinnamon".

Operating out of Sri Lanka's renowned cinnamon belt, our company works hand-in-hand with experienced local farming families. We manage every single stage—from shoot selection and delicate hand-peeling to climate-controlled storage and customs clearance.

Our Vision

To share the best of Sri Lankan cinnamon with the world.

Our Mission

To promote natural wellness by delivering authentic Ceylon cinnamon, rootek in Sri Lankan tradition, that enhance health, flavor and quality of life globally.

Educational Insights

Ceylon Cinnamon vs Cassia Cinnamon

Why international buyers specifically demand pure Sri Lankan Ceylon Cinnamon

Feature Ceylon Cinnamon (Zelan) Cassia Cinnamon (Ordinary)
Botanical Name Cinnamomum Verum Cinnamomum Cassia
Coumarin Content < 0.004% (Ultra-Low / Safe) Up to 1.0% (Toxic in large doses)
Quill Structure Soft, multi-layered fragile paper-thin rolls Thick, hard, hollow single layer bark
Flavor Profile Delicate, sweet, complex citrus notes Pungent, harsh, bitter spice notes
